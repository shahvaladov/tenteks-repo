Motorized Awning System

Motorized awning is a robust system then can be used in all weather conditions and works with a special tensioning system. Their structures are long–lasting and are adaptable to all kinds of spaces.

Motorized awning systems can be used in outer space’s of the cafes and restaurants also in gardens and terrace’s of the residences in all seasons.

With it’s high quality three-layer fabric motorized awning systems protects from the summer heat, sun’s harmful rays, rain and the impact of severe weather conditions. Rails and moving parts do not make sound and do not require maintenance. With remote control unit, motorized awning system brings you a very practical use due to it’s rectractible feature.

Closed spaces can be created by applying various covering systems with motorized awning systems. By this way your outdoor space becomes an open environment in summer heats; Also it becomes a closed space in severe weather conditions in winter. Motorized awning systems brings you outdoor fun in 4 seasons.

Motor & Automation

Somfy and Becker motors are used in Automatic Awning Systems and they can be controlled by remote control. You can track your commands from the Hi-Tech digital screen and set your opening and closing time with this remote control. With parallel connection feature you can integrate the system via wireless without central operating system relay. Anti-jam feature automatically stops the system when an obstacle is encountered. This is a security feature for the sake of the system. With high torque (120Nm) lift, system provides maximum streching feature in the fabric. Remote control system has a unique frequency feature (868 Mhz) that prevents any kind of mixing with other frequencies and it has a very simple channel selection and usage interface (r20/17psf).

Becker and Somfy motors provides full automation for wind sensors, sun sensors, radio receivers for dimmers (led light level dimming) and for other automation products..With this optional features your awning could have a full automation.

If the weather is too hot you can open your awning by remote control easily.  Whether you wish to turn out or turn on the lights you can control the lightening with your remote control. With your intelligent remote control you will be ready for every kind of weather conditions. In windy, rainy or cloudy weathers, your awning will automatically know what to do. With this features, your system will not take any damages in severe weather conditions.

With B-Tronic tablet everything will be very easy and user friendly. B-Tronic tablet will let you control your awnings, becker automated shutters, becker automated curtains, power sockets with becker receivers and all devices that are KNX based via internet from your smart phone, tablet and laptop.

Technical Details

RTS 1300 RAIL PROFILE

Our RTS 1300 series rail profile dimension is 140mm X 70mm.

This profile weight is 3300 gr. per meter.

The raw material of our high alloyed extrusion profile is T5 6063 aluminum.

The rail profile has the strength to be used up to 6 meters without any steel or tension reinforcement.

RTS 1300 RAIN GUTTER PROFILE

RTS 1300 series rain gutter profile dimension is 140mm X 140mm.

This profile weight is 4800 gr per meter.

T5 6063 aluminum is the raw material of our high alloyed extrusion profiles.

This profile collects rainwater and discharges water through the posts attached to the lower part.

In addition, the front girder strength is an element that gives extra strength to the structure.

Channels on the profile provides ease of disassembly application of the system

RTS 1300 PRE-STRUT PROFILE

Our RTS 1300 series pre-strut dimension is 100mm X 100mm.

This profile weight is 3650 gr per meter.

The raw material of our high alloyed extrusion profile is T5 6063 aluminum.

It is mounted on the channels at the bottom of the rain gutter profile and drains rainwater to the ground level.

The channels inside the strut profile helps to disassemble the system and are also designed to hide the screw density.

RTS 1300 FABRIC & STRETCHING PROFILES

Our stretching fabric profile weight is 1800 gr per meter.

This profile is used in the front and rear part of the fabric awning and with the strength of its structure and the power of the motor, stretching fabric profile opens and closes the fabric easily and gives you a maximum stretching feature.

Also our fabric profile weight is 1000 gr per meter.

In order to increase the bearing capacity of the system these profiles applied to the structure in specific intervals according to project’s dynamics.

The raw material of our high alloyed extrusion profile is T5 6063 aluminum.

RTS 1300 BEAM PROFILE AND GLASS BEARING PROFILE

RTS1300 series beam profile dimension is 135mm x 30mm.

Beam profile weight is 1745 gr per meter.

The glass bearing profile dimension is 150mm x 50mm.

Glass bearing profile weight is 2150 gr per meter.

Alternative glass bearing profile dimension is 120mm x 60mm.

And the weight of the profile is 1900 gr per meter.

T5 6063 aluminum is the raw material of our high alloy extrusion profile.
